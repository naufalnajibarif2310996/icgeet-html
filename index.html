WELCOME TO ICGEET 2025!

It is with great pleasure we welcome you to the 2025 International Conference on Green Energy and Environmental Technology (ICGEET) in Bandung, Indonesia.

ICGEET aims to bring together networking among international researchers, engineers, and practitioners, providing a platform for the exchange of ideas and collaboration to advance green energy and environmental technology. We look forward to your participation!

ICGEET 2025 will be held in Bandung, Indonesia, August 14, 2025. The conference will be held at Travello Hotel Bandung, which is located in the heart of Bandung, Indonesia, which is known as the "Paris of Java" due to its beautiful scenery and charming atmosphere

CALL FOR PAPERS

We invite all academics, researchers, professionals, and students to participate in ICGEET 2025. Please see the Submission page for further details.
