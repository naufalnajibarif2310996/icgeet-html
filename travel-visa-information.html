Find Out if You Need a Visa

Check if your country is part of the visa on arrival (VoA) or free visa subject list here. If your country is not on the list, you need to apply for a visa at an Indonesian embassy to enter Indonesia. Please follow the steps below:

  1. Request your visa letter of invitation. Letters of invitation to ICA 2025 are only available if you have an in-person registration.
  2. Submit your visa application. See the details below.

Visa Application

If you need a visa, apply online via the official e-visa website. We recommend applying for a C11 visa (single-entry visit visa for conferences and seminars). Documents required for the visa application are listed below:

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months before it expires
  • Personal bank statement with a minimum amount of USD 2000 or
  • equivalent for the last 3 months
  • Recent color photograph
  • Proof of guarantee from the guarantor
  • Letter of invitation from ICA 2025’s organizer
    • This visa typically takes 5-10 working days to process from the date of application submission. For more information about the visa, visit the official website of the Immigration of the Republic of Indonesia.

    Information for VoA Holders

    Near the ICA 2025 venue, the points of entry for VoA holders are:

  • Soekarno-Hatta International Airport, Banten
  • Halim Perdanakusuma International Airport, DKI Jakarta
  • Kertajati International Airport, West Java
  • I Gusti Ngurah Rai International Airport, Bali
    • At the immigration checkpoint, you need to present the following documents:
  • Passport valid for at least 6 months before it expires
  • Copy of a return or connecting ticket
  • Proof of VoA payment

    • If Your Visa is Denied

    See our cancellation policy for information on how to proceed.
