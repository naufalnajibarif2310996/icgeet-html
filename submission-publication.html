Publication Details

Selected papers presented at ICGEET 2025 will be considered for publication in the following journals and proceedings. Each publication has its own scope, editorial standards, and indexing status.

IOP Conference Series: Earth and Environmental Science*

A prestigious series indexed in Scopus and Web of Science that covers physics, engineering, and energy topics. All submissions will undergo a rigorous peer-review process in accordance with AIP standards.

Visit Journal Page
Publication Fee: *

Jurnal Penelitian dan Pendidikan

An open-access peer-reviewed journal publishing research in education, pedagogy, and teaching practices in science and engineering education contexts.

Visit Journal Page
Publication Fee: Rp 1.000.000
International Indexed

Indonesia Journal of Teaching in Science

Focuses on innovative methods, curriculum development, and assessment practices related to science teaching in primary, secondary, and tertiary education.

Visit Journal Page
Publication Fee: *

Jurnal ABMAS

Covers applied community service and development programs based on scientific research and sustainable practices in energy, technology, and environment.

Visit Journal Page
Publication Fee: Rp 500.000
International Indexed

Indonesian Review of Community Service

Publishes peer-reviewed articles that highlight impactful community outreach activities, focusing on environmental education, renewable energy, and rural empowerment.

Visit Journal Page
Publication Fee: Free

Jurnal Pendidikan Teknologi Informasi dan Komunikasi

Covers research and development in ICT-based education, digital learning environments, and emerging technologies in teaching.

Visit Journal Page
Publication Fee: Free

Journal of Science Learning

Publishes high-quality research on science learning, including cognitive development, conceptual understanding, and learning environments

Visit Journal Page
Publication Fee: Rp 1.000.000

Elkawnie: Journal of Islamic Science and Technology

Integrates Islamic perspectives into research in science and technology. Covers themes on green innovation, ethical engineering, and sustainable development with Islamic values.

Visit Journal Page
Publication Fee: Rp 1.000.000
